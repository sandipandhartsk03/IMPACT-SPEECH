Welcome!

IMPACT-SPEECH is a full-day workshop focused on identifying, measuring, preventing, and assessing bias in speech-enabled large language models and multimodal speech systems.

Co-located with EMNLP 2026 • Full-Day Workshop

Workshop Overview

Recent advances in multimodal learning have accelerated the transition from text-based systems to speech-enabled interfaces. This rapid adoption has outpaced our understanding of limitations, biases, and societal consequences of speech-based large language models.

IMPACT-SPEECH brings together researchers from speech processing, NLP, machine learning, social sciences, and ethics to systematically analyze, characterize, and mitigate bias in speech-based AI systems.

Workshop Themes

  • Types and Interactions of Bias: Identifying accent, gender, speaker identity and socioeconomic bias in speech systems.
  • Sources of Bias: Bias introduced through datasets, annotation processes, training objectives and deployment environments.
  • Bias Mitigation Strategies: General mitigation strategies and bias-specific approaches in multilingual and cross-lingual speech models.
  • Real-World Consequences: How biased speech systems affect access to technology, employment opportunities and human-AI interaction.
